Remember what you read online.

Archive quietly saves posts you spend time on — then lets you search them later. Private, local, no account required.

Add to Chrome Get update emails

Works on

LinkedIn · Instagram · TikTok · Google Search

Privacy

  • Memories stay on your device (IndexedDB).
  • No cloud account. No ads. No data sold.
  • Optional local backend for power users only.

1 · Browse

Use LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, or Google like normal.

2 · Pause

Stay on a post for 2 seconds, or tap Capture visible in the popup.

3 · Search

Open Archive and search by keyword, author, or platform anytime.
